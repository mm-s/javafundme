Carlos y Elian

Please allow me to introduce him; he’s a man of wealth and taste... but...

On 11 August, a wildfire in Tres Cantos (Madrid) destroyed the home that Carlos had built through years of effort. Carlos is a devoted father to Elian, who has severe autism due to MEF2C syndrome, and they urgently need a safe, adapted place to live. Donations will fund emergency housing, essentials, and the first steps to rebuild so they can regain stability together.
